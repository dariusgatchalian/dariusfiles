COURSES WE OFFERED




Welcome to the Senior High and College programs at PhilTech GMA! Our institution offers a wide range of courses designed to meet the diverse needs and interests of our students. In our Senior High program, students can choose from various academic tracks, such as ABM, HUMSS, and GAS, which provide a strong foundation for college and career readiness. Our College program offers bachelor's degree programs in fields such as BSCS (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science), BSOA (Bachelor of Science in Office Administration), Business Administration, and BTVTED (Bachelor in Technical-Vocational Teacher Education), among others. With experienced faculty, modern facilities, and industry-relevant curriculum, our courses are designed to prepare students for the challenges of the real world and empower them to excel in their chosen fields. Join us and unlock your potential for success!
